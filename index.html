Soul Space Chennai™ is a safe and calm space created for people to express their emotions freely. By being part of this space, you agree to be respectful and mindful in all interactions.

Participation is strictly limited to individuals who are 18 years or older. This ensures a mature and emotionally safe environment.

Each session or gathering is limited to around 18 to 20 people to maintain a personal and meaningful experience. Entry is only based on prior confirmation.

Use of alcohol, drugs, or any intoxicating substances is strictly not allowed under any circumstances.

This is not a medical, therapy, or counseling service. It is a peer-based emotional support space.

All participants are expected to treat each other with respect. Any inappropriate behavior may lead to removal.

While we encourage openness, complete confidentiality cannot be legally guaranteed. Please share responsibly.

Each individual is responsible for their own emotions, actions, and decisions. Soul Space Chennai™ is not liable for personal outcomes.

This platform is not meant for emergency or crisis situations. Please seek professional help if needed.
