Soul Space is a space for emotional expression and human connection.

Only individuals aged 18 years and above are allowed to participate.

Gatherings are intentionally limited to 18–20 people to maintain emotional safety and comfort.

Entry is strictly based on prior confirmation.

Alcohol, drugs, or any harmful substances are strictly prohibited.

Respect for others’ emotions, silence, and personal boundaries is mandatory at all times.

No recording, photography, or sharing of personal expressions outside the space is allowed.

This space does not provide therapy, counseling, or any medical support.

Soul Space is not suitable for emergency or crisis situations.

Any form of disrespectful behavior will result in immediate removal from the space.

Participants are fully responsible for their own emotional well-being during and after the gathering.

Confidentiality must be maintained at all times without exception.

By choosing to be part of Soul Space, you agree to honor the safety, privacy, and trust of everyone present.
