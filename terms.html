Soul Space is a space for emotional expression and human connection.

Only individuals aged 18 years and above are allowed.

Gatherings are limited to 18–20 people to maintain a safe and personal environment.

Entry is strictly based on prior confirmation.

Alcohol, drugs, or any harmful substances are strictly prohibited.

Respect for others' emotions, silence, and boundaries is mandatory.

No recording, photography, or sharing of personal experiences is allowed.

Everything shared within the space must remain confidential.

This is not a therapy, counseling, or medical support space.

Not suitable for emergency or crisis situations.

Any disrespectful or harmful behavior will result in removal.

Participants are responsible for their own emotional well-being.

By being part of Soul Space, you agree to maintain trust, safety, and privacy.
